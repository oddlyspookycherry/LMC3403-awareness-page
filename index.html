GT Archive Student Outreach

What is GT Archive and Why is it Important?

Students are the heart of the Georgia Tech community, but the University Archives consist mostly of academic and administrative records. To create a more complete and inclusive history of campus life based on student’s experiences, student organizations are encouraged to donate their inactive records to the University Archives. These contributions are vital in showcasing the history of Georgia Tech from a student’s point of view.

Records from student organizations, including fraternities, cultural groups, and special interest clubs, form an increasingly valuable part of the Archives. By sharing your organization’s history, you help ensure that the unique contributions of Georgia Tech’s student body are preserved for future generations.
